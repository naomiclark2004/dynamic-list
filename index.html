Best Places to Visit in Arizona

Arizona is "The Grand Canyon State," and yes, nothing else in the world is quite like Arizona's most famous natural wonder. But you'll also find other types of spectacular natural beauty: alpine tundra, evergreen forests, riparian canyons, snow-capped peaks, and flower-carpeted flatlands. Despite being dubbed the Grand Canyon State, Arizona's wild and varied beauty is often underrated—with too few people reveling in its snow capped mountains, towering saguaro cacti, and glimmering waterfalls that extend far beyond the iconic canyon.
